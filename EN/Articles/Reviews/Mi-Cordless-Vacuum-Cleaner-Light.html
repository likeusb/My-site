Mi Cordless Vacuum Cleaner Light Review

Sep 4, 2023

  • Suction power
  • Battery life
  • Build quality
  • Pricing

Suction power

Every measure of how powerful a vacuum is is just straight up awful. There is no universal measurement and some use kPa, some use AW, some use some other garbage. The conversions don't exist and it's a mess so I'll just use normal, human terms.

It's good.

It's enough to clean out a carpet quite well, too. Compared to my other vacuum, a parkside dry vacuum that weighs half a ton and is very loud, it performs similarly on carpet and a little worse on regular floor. Still, good enough for daily, non intensive use.

Just don't vacuum water with it otherwise it might be ruined.

Battery life

Battery life is great!
45 minutes on regular mode (which is what I use most of the time) is more than enough to get my entire 2 floor many room house cleaned in one go and it's a huge convenience since whenever I want to vacuum, I just pick it up from the charging station, pick up the nozzles I'll need (usually the corner one works best) and go to vacuuming. The one downside is 5 hours to charge 0-100 & no replacable batteries.

Build quality

This is where the first problem occurs. I don't know why, but the build quality is good (very repairable), but the tolerances on the connectors are AWFUL. Though, outside of this problem (That I rarely see anyways), it's quite good.

Price

Finally, the price.
It's 110€ and I got it for sale for just 80€.
I paid an extra 16€ after a year to replace the HEPA filter and will do so bi-annually for 16€/year (2 pack).

Overall ratings & recommendation:

  • Suction power: 4/5
  • Battery life: 4/5
  • Build Quality: 3/5
  • Price: 5/5
  • Overall: 4/5
  • Recommended? Absolutely.
